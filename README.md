# 🕌 Hyderabad Travel Itinerary: A 4-Day Vegetarian Guide

Welcome to your 4-day Hyderabad itinerary! This guide is perfectly tailored for a mix of modern city sights, historic wonders, and the absolute best vegetarian eats the city has to offer. 

## 📅 Overview
* **Dates:** March 14 – March 17
* **Focus:** Sightseeing & Vegetarian Culinary Highlights
* **Transport Modes:** Metro, Auto-rickshaws, and Cabs

---

## 🌆 March 14: Modern Hyderabad 
**Time:** 5:00 PM – Night  
*Explore the glittering skyline and modern infrastructure of Hitech City.*

### 📍 Places to Visit
* **Durgam Cheruvu Cable Bridge:** Walk the illuminated bridge for a stunning view of the Hitech City skyline.
* **Inorbit Mall:** Great for a quick stroll or some retail therapy.

### 🍽️ Vegetarian Food Stops
* **Taaza Kitchen (Madhapur):** Stand-and-eat style. Highly recommend the *Ghee Podi Masala Dosa* and *Filter Coffee*.
* **Cream Stone (Hitech City):** Perfect for late-night "cold stone" ice cream creations.

### 🚕 Travel
* Short Auto/Cab from ESCI (~15 mins).

---

## 🛥️ March 15: Lakes & Temples
**Time:** 5:00 PM – Night  
*Experience the serene side of Hyderabad as the sun sets.*

### 📍 Places to Visit
* **Hussain Sagar Lake:** Take a sunset boat ride to the Monolithic Buddha Statue.
* **Birla Mandir:** A stunning white marble temple on a hill; visit at twilight for the best city views.

### 🍽️ Vegetarian Food Stops
* **Chutneys (Banjara Hills/Panjagutta):** Iconic for its *Steam Dosa* served with six different varieties of chutney.
* **Niloufer Cafe:** End the night with their famous *Irani Chai* and *Malai Bun*.

### 🚇 Travel
* Take the Metro from **Raidurg** to **Ameerpet**, then switch to the Red Line for **Lakdi-ka-pul**.

---

## 🏰 March 16: Heritage & History
**Time:** Morning – Night  
*Dive deep into the royal past of the Nizams in the Old City.*

### 📍 Places to Visit
* **Salar Jung Museum:** View the famous Musical Clock and the Veiled Rebecca statue.
* **Chowmahalla Palace:** Explore the grand Durbar Hall and an impressive vintage car collection.
* **Charminar & Laad Bazaar:** Walk the historic gates and shop for beautiful bangles and pearls.

### 🍽️ Vegetarian Food Stops
* **Govind’s Dosa (Charminar):** Famous for their *Butter Masala Dosa* (generously drenched in butter).
* **Nimrah Cafe:** An essential stop for *Irani Chai* and *Osmania Biscuits* overlooking the Charminar.
* **Santosh Dhaba Exclusive (Abids):** Sit down for an excellent *Veg Hyderabadi Biryani* and *Paneer Butter Masala*.

### 🚇 Travel
* Take the Metro to **MGBS Station**, then catch an Auto to navigate the Old City sites.

---

## 🛡️ March 17: Royal Forts
**Time:** Morning – 6:00 PM  
*Conclude your trip with the architectural marvels of the Qutb Shahi dynasty.*

### 📍 Places to Visit
* **Golconda Fort:** Spend 2-3 hours exploring the acoustic wonders and the hilltop citadel.
* **Qutb Shahi Tombs:** Peaceful, grand mausoleums located just 1km from the fort.

### 🍽️ Vegetarian Food Stops
* **Minerva Coffee Shop (Somajiguda/Himayatnagar):** Treat yourself to a classic *South Indian Thali* or *Button Idlis in Sambar*.
* **Subhan Bakery (Nampally):** Pick up *Dum-ke-Roat* (delicious baked cookies) to take home as a souvenir.

### 🚕 Travel
* Best reached via Cab/Auto from Gachibowli (~25 mins).
